About Author

Gege Akutami (芥あくた見み下げ々げ Akutami Gege?) is a Japanese manga artist and the author of Jujutsu Kaisen. In 2018, Gege Akutami started their first full serialization with Jujutsu Kaisen in issue 14 of Weekly Shonen Jump. Jujutsu Kaisen serves as the follow-up series to Jujutsu Kaisen 0: Jujutsu High.
